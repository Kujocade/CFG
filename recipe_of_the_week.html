Sambal prawns

Ingredients

  • 20 raw Prawns
  • 25g dessicated coconut
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 red chillis
  • 200g green beans
  • 1 tbsp kecap manis
  • 60g cashew nuts
  • brown sugar
  • sea salt

Method

Preheat the wok and add oil. Cook the prawns in a single layer until they have turned pink. Set them aside on a plate.

Add a little more oil to the wok. Add garlic, chili, green beans, kecap manis and salt. Cook until the green beans start to soften.

Add the coconut and cashews then return the prawns to the wok. Toss it together so it is mixed.

Serve in a bowl with some extra cashews for garnish. You can have it with jasmine rice to make a bigger meal.
