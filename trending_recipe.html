Butternut squash & tahini

Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash
  • 400g chickpeas
  • 2 thyme springs
  • 60g tahini
  • 80g Greek yoghurt
  • 1 tsp honey
  • squeeze of lemon juice
  • pinch of paprika
  • a few coriander leaves

Method

Preheat the oven to 180. Cut the squash in to pieces and place in a roasting tin with some olive oil for 20 minutes.

While you wait you can make the dressing. Whisk tahini, honey and yoghurt. Then add coriander, parsley, paprika, salt, pepper and lemon juice.

After the 20 minutes, add chickpeas to the roasting tin and cook for 10 minutes.

Once cooked, put the roasted squash and chickpeas on to a plate and spoon the dressing over it. To make it beautiful you could add some seeds on top. Serve with crusty bread.
